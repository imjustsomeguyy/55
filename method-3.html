Amazon ~£140 Limit Refund Method

amazon.com
  1. Use an account that is aged at least 2 months without any refunds already on it
  2. Find an item off Amazon that is £130 or under (if you want to refund an amount higher than this check the method list)
  3. Make sure it's not an apple product, make sure it's dispatched by amazon
  4. Order this item to an Amazon hub locker near you instead of your address
  5. Wait for it to arrive
  6. When it's arrived go onto Amazons live chat, tell them your order number etc, if you can't get onto live chat just phone them, works the same
  7. Then tell them your confused because your parcel was empty (parcel, not hub locker)
  8. They may ask a few questions, if they ask if it was damaged, say no, if they ask if it was sealed say yes
  9. You may have to fill a form out, it's rare but sometimes they ask, also sometimes for ID, if they do just give. Enjoy the refund
